About Me

Welcome to my mini portfolio website. My name is Anitha. I live in Durham NH. I am passionate about pottery and i'm a creative thinker. For two year's I have learnt pottery at "Red Door pottery" studio in Maine. I have five year's of experience in this field. I have a few of handmade pottery pieces.
